Geoprism.ai is an application suite for creating and maintaining interoperable Spatial Knowledge Graphs (iSKGs) optimized for Geo-GraphRAG workflows. It implements the Common Geo-Registry (CGR) approach, treating locations, location hierarchies, and networks of locations as cross-sector semantic standards. This enables siloed geospatial datasets—from NSDIs, digital public goods, and domain systems—to be aligned and integrated into a cohesive geospatial knowledge layer that Large Language Models can query and reason over.
